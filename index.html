Lucky Adventures – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Lucky Adventures is a private app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Storage

All data including ratings, profiles, and activity history is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Camera Permission

The app requests camera access for document scanning functionality using Google ML Kit. Images are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded or shared.

Biometric Data

The app uses biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition) for security. Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system and never accessed or stored by the app.

WiFi Sync

The app offers optional local WiFi sync between two paired devices. Data is transmitted directly between devices on the same network only. No data passes through external servers.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us.
